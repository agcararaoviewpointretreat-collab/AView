Discover Your Perfect Haven

Step into a world where rustic charm meets modern elegance at Agcararao Mountain View Resort.

Thoughtfully curated spaces combine local charm with modern comforts for a serene retreat. Imagine waking to panoramic views of lush landscapes, crowned by an ethereal sea of clouds at dawn.

Casa De Amor

Escape to Casa De Amor, where breathtaking mountain views, misty mornings, and a sea of clouds create the perfect peaceful retreat. This charming A-frame bamboo hut offers a cozy escape, fresh mountain air, and stunning panoramic landscapes. Relax on the hammock net balcony, watch the sunrise, or unwind in nature’s embrace.

Casa De Bella

Escape to Casa De Bella, a charming bamboo cabin nestled in the heart of Agcararao Mountain View Resort, Passi City. Relax in a beautifully crafted bamboo-inspired cabin featuring spacious interiors, cozy native furniture, and an open-concept design that allows you to fully embrace the surrounding natural beauty.

Casa De Arced

Escape to Casa De Arced, a serene haven tucked away in nature’s embrace. This enchanting bamboo-inspired hideaway blends rustic charm with modern comforts, offering a truly authentic retreat. Start your mornings with breathtaking mountain vistas as you savor a cup of coffee on your spacious private balcony. Let the gentle whispers of the breeze and the melodies of birdsong create a tranquil escape from the everyday hustle.

Experience a relaxing stay in Casa Y Agua, our traditional bamboo cottage designed with a blend of native charm and modern comfort. Built with nipa and bamboo materials, this spacious open-air cottage offers a refreshing tropical vibe perfect for family and barkada getaways.
