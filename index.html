The Journey

Agcararao Mountain View Resort, located in Sitio Agcararao, Barangay Jaguimitan, Passi City, Iloilo, is a relatively recent addition to the region's tourist attractions. Established by the family of former Binibining Pintados PSSG Jo-nah Belle Capuy-Arcenal, the resort was developed to showcase the area's natural beauty, including its flower and vegetable farms, panoramic views, and the phenomenon known as the "sea of clouds". The resort officially opened to the public on February 18, 2020, offering visitors opportunities for trekking, relaxation, and photography amidst scenic landscapes. Since its inauguration, Agcararao Mountain View has become a favored destination for both locals and tourists seeking a tranquil escape in Iloilo's highlands.
