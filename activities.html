Unleash your spirit of exploration and enjoy stunning experiences at Agcararao Mountain View Resort.

Explore & Enjoy

ATV Adventure

Get ready for an adrenaline-pumping adventure at Agcararao Mountain View Resort! Hop on an ATV and navigate through rugged trails, scenic mountain paths, and breathtaking landscapes. Feel the thrill of the ride while soaking in the fresh mountain air and stunning panoramic views.

Rainbow Slide

Experience the ultimate thrill as you glide down the vibrant Rainbow Slide at Agcararao Mountain View Resort! This colorful, high-speed slide offers an exciting ride with breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains. Perfect for all ages, it's a must-try attraction for those seeking fun and adventure.

Rates

₱75 (1 drop) ₱100 (2 drops)

Pool Games

Enjoy a unique twist on classic billiards with our wooden puck billiards table at Agcararao Mountain View Resort. Set against a breathtaking mountain backdrop, this game offers a fun and exciting challenge for all skill levels. Play, relax, and take in the fresh air while making unforgettable memories with friends and family.

Rates

₱100 (per hour)

Billiards

Unwind and enjoy a friendly game of billiards at our indoor recreation area. Whether you're a seasoned player or just looking for some casual fun, our billiards area offers the perfect spot to relax and challenge friends or family to an exciting match.

Rates

₱100 (per hour)

Bonfire

Gather around the warm glow of a bonfire at Agcararao Mountain View Resort and experience the perfect way to unwind under the starry sky. Enjoy the crackling flames, cozy ambiance, and breathtaking mountain views while sharing stories, roasting marshmallows, or simply soaking in the peaceful atmosphere.
